Polygon

A polygon is a closed, two-dimensional shape composed of straight line segments called sides, which meet at points known as vertices. Polygons are classified based on the number of sides, such as triangles with three sides, quadrilaterals with four sides, pentagons with five sides, and so on. They can be further categorized as regular polygons, where all sides and angles are equal, or irregular polygons, where sides and angles differ.
